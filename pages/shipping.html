Shipping Policy for Paradigma Games

1. Order Accuracy and Changes

Prior to finalizing your order, please ensure the accuracy of your order details and shipping address. While we strive to accommodate changes, post-order edits may not always be feasible. If adjustments are necessary, promptly contact william@paradigma.games, and we will make every effort to assist you.

2. Shipping Costs

Shipping rates are determined based on package weight and destination. During checkout, the corresponding shipping charges and estimated delivery times for your order will be calculated and displayed.

3. Dispatch Timing

Typically, orders are dispatched within 2-5 business days, unless otherwise specified on the website header or product description. During peak periods like the holiday season, dispatch times may extend due to increased order volume. You will receive a notification email when your order is prepared for shipment.

4. Delivery Schedule

Paradigma Games ships to various countries from global fulfillment centers, resulting in varying shipping times. Refer to your order confirmation email for the estimated delivery time post-dispatch. Keep in mind that delivery times are estimations and subject to variations influenced by factors such as distance, local holidays, and external circumstances beyond Paradigma Games and our fulfillment partners' control.

5. Order Issues

If you suspect your order is lost, arrives damaged, or if you have any concerns, reach out to william@paradigma.games promptly. We are here to address and resolve any issues to ensure your satisfaction.
